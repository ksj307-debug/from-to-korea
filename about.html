CEO / Our Story

Bridging Worlds Through Authentic Experiences

After eight transformative years living abroad, I returned to my roots with a profound new perspective. I understood deeply what travelers seek—the desire not just to see a place, but to feel its heartbeat, to understand its nuances without the friction of typical tourism.

As a native Korean with a deep, abiding love for my country, I founded From To Korea to share the 'Real Local Seoul.' We bypass the crowded, generic routes in favor of intimate, authentic encounters.

Our philosophy is simple: we design journeys that feel less like a guided tour and more like an exclusive introduction to the city by a knowledgeable, well-connected local friend.
