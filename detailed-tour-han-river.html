🌟 About This Experience

Want to experience the iconic Han River picnic just like you’ve seen in K-dramas, but feeling overwhelmed by heavy gear rentals or complicated Korean delivery apps? We’ve got you covered!

This is not just a rental service; it’s a fully curated, hassle-free lifestyle experience. Your local host will set up a picture-perfect, Instagrammable picnic spot for you, order the best local delivery food, and share insider travel tips. Once the setup is done, the space is entirely yours to enjoy the sunset, city lights, and ultimate relaxation. When you're done, simply walk away—we’ll handle the clean-up!
